General Questions

What is SkillSwap ?

SkillSwap is a platform that allows users to exchange and share their skills, facilitating collaboration between people with similar interests.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button at the top of the page, fill out the registration form, and follow the instructions.

Questions about Skills

How do I add my skills?

After logging in, go to your profile and click on "Add Skill". Fill in the details and save your changes.

Can I learn more than one skill at once?

Yes, you can search for and participate in multiple skills exchanges at the same time, depending on your availability.

Support and Assistance

What if I have a problem with another user?

Contact our support team via the "Contact Us" page and provide the necessary details. We will do our best to resolve the issue.

How can I contact technical support?

You can reach us at support@skillswap.com or via our contact form available on the website.
