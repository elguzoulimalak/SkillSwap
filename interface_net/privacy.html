Terms and services

Introduction

When you use our services, you trust us to handle your information. We know this is a significant responsibility, which is why we strive to protect it while giving you control over it.

Data Collection

The information we collect is used to improve the services we provide to all our users. This may include basic information, such as the language you use, or more complex data like the people you are most interested in on the web. The information we collect and how we use it depends on how you use our services and manage your privacy settings. When you are signed in to your account, we store the collected information by associating it with your Google account and consider it as personal information.

Use and Sharing of Data

Many SkillSwap services allow you to share your information with others and control how it is shared. For example, you can share your codes publicly or ensure they remain private. Keep in mind that if you share information publicly, your content may become accessible through search engines.

Data Security

SkillSwap integrates powerful security features that continuously protect your information. The data we collect through our services enables us to automatically detect and block threats to your security. If we detect significant suspicious activity, we will inform you and provide advice to enhance your security. We implement all necessary security measures to protect you, as well as SkillSwap, from any unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of the information we hold.
